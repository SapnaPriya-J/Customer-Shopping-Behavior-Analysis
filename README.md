# Customer-Shopping-Behavior-Analysis
Why customer shopping behavior analyzing is important for businesses?

Understanding customer shopping behavior enables organizations to make data-driven decisions that enhance revenue growth, improve customer retention, and optimize operational strategies.

As part of my project analyzing 3,900+ customer transactions, I derived actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

 Business Impact Delivered:

• Revenue Optimization – Identified high-value customers and top revenue-contributing age segments for targeted marketing.

• Customer Retention Strategy – Segmented customers into New, Returning, and Loyal categories to support loyalty program design.

• Marketing Effectiveness – Analyzed subscriber vs. non-subscriber spending patterns to strengthen subscription strategies.

• Discount Efficiency – Evaluated discount-dependent products to balance profitability and sales growth.

• Product Strategy – Identified top-performing and highest-rated products for improved positioning.

• Operational Insights – Assessed the impact of shipping types on purchase behavior.

Technical Skills Applied:

• Python (Data Cleaning & Preparation)

Turning transactional data into measurable business value through analytics.
